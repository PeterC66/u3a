Christmas round up 4 Many groups enjoy a get-together at Christmas. Here are just a few of the events that took place in December. Petanque Over 60 members and partners sat down for our lunch at a very festive St Ives Golf Club. We all took part in a Christmas quiz and enjoyed food from a varied menu. We were entertained by Carol, Grace and Maggie singing a song written by Carol to the tune of ‘All Things Bright and Beautiful’. There was a lucky draw where the winners won French wine or cheese. The Golf Club looked after us superbly and we look forward to returning next year. Ron Meadows Swedish Gill from the Swedish Group prepared a ‘julbodet’ (Christmas feast) for the group at her home. They enjoyed plenty of Swedish food and games. Pia Consoli Art Appreciation The Art Appreciation group enjoyed a not-so-festive lunch at The Tap Room, but quite delicious all the same and lots of fun. Anne Bannell Table Tennis We had our Christmas lunch at Slepe Hall. Twenty-one mem- bers attended and an enjoyable afternoon was had with lovely food, great company and a few quizzes to keep everyone entertained. Christine Blackmore Philosophy Group The philosophy group held its Christmas party in the Axe and Compass where everyone had a jolly time ﬁguring out some puzzles and a Christmas themed quiz. The group reﬂected on a successful year and that planning was in hand to produce some original seminars and the play they are putting on in the Corn Exchange on 6th September. A good time was had by all. Roy Moyser
